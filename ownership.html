SalesBets Ownership Model

The NSBLPA follows the innovative SalesBets ownership structure that allows professionals to invest in teams and share in their success.

Revenue Sharing

Owners receive a percentage of team revenues from betting operations, sponsorships, and merchandise.

Strategic Decision Making

Owners participate in key decisions regarding team management, player acquisitions, and business development.

Scalable Investment

Multiple investment tiers are available to accommodate different budget levels and commitment.

Benefits of Ownership

Exclusive access to team operations and data
Networking opportunities with industry leaders
Potential for significant financial returns
Brand exposure and marketing opportunities
Participation in a growing sports entertainment sector
